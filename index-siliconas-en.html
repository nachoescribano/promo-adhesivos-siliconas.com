NEW RANGE OF SILICONES

Sealing, insulating and applicable to different indoor and outdoor surfaces

The INDEX® range of silicones is available in a variety of colours and properties to adapt to the requirements of each application. All of the silicones are water-resistant and can adapt to different materials, making them a professional solution for the assembly and sealing of joints on different surfaces.
